# L3 Specialist Certification

**Job Value Range:** $800 - $1,100 per job
**Prerequisites:** L2 Technician + 6 months documented field experience
**Written Exam:** 40 questions, 80% passing, 75 minutes
**Practical:** Required

## What L3 Unlocks

- Multi-platform assignments (Unitree G1, H1-2, Boston Dynamics Spot, DJI Agras T50)
- FFT-based vibration analysis for bearing fault identification
- Fleet-level MTBF analysis and predictive maintenance scheduling
- Advanced FMEA with RPN scoring and risk escalation authority
- AR Mode diagnostic sessions as lead technician

## Estimated Study Time

- Self-study: 50-70 hours
- Lab time: 20-25 hours
- Total: 70-95 hours after passing L2
