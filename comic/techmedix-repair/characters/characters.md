# Character Definitions - TechMedix: The Repair

**Style**: ohmsha preset (manga + neutral + webtoon)
**Art Direction**: Old-school anime / manga, clean lines, expressive faces

---

## Character 1: The Owner (Alex)

**Role**: Student / Customer (Role A - represents the reader)
**Age**: Late 20s to early 30s

**Appearance**:
- Face shape: Slightly round, friendly
- Hair: Messy short brown hair
- Eyes: Large, expressive brown eyes
- Build: Average height, casual posture
- Distinguishing features: Perpetually a little flustered, expressive eyebrows

**Costume**:
- Default outfit: Casual hoodie (light gray/blue), jeans, sneakers
- Color palette: Muted blues (#4A90D9), light gray (#D1D5DB), warm tones
- Accessories: Smartphone always in hand

**Expression Range**:
- Neutral: Normal, approachable
- Panic/Frustrated: Sweat drops, wide eyes, furrowed brows
- Confused: Tilted head, question mark above, hand to chin
- Relieved/Grateful: Soft smile, sparkle in eyes, relaxed posture

---

## Character 2: The TechMedix Agent (Kai)

**Role**: Mentor / Expert (Role B - the knowledgeable technician)
**Age**: 30s

**Appearance**:
- Face shape: Angular, confident jaw
- Hair: Tidy dark hair, swept back
- Eyes: Sharp, focused eyes with a knowing glint
- Build: Tall, confident posture
- Distinguishing features: Calm, professional demeanor; always seems in control

**Costume**:
- Default outfit: BlackCat Robotics / TechMedix uniform — dark navy jumpsuit with TechMedix logo patch on chest, utility belt with tool pouches, and a small diagnostic tablet
- Color palette: Navy (#1B2838), orange accents (#FF6B35) for the Brand, white trim
- Accessories: Diagnostic tablet/holographic projector, multi-tool, robot-repair gauntlets

**Expression Range**:
- Neutral: Professional, calm
- Focused: Squinting eyes, analyzing
- Reassuring: Gentle smile, "I've got this" energy
- Triumphant: Confident grin when the robot boots up

---

## Character 3: The Humanoid Robot (AR-7)

**Role**: Challenge / The "Patient" (Role C - the problem to solve)
**"Age"**: Latest model, still relatively new

**Appearance**:
- Face shape: Sleek, rounded humanoid head with minimalist anime-style face
- "Hair": None — smooth white/gray casing
- Eyes: LED-style eyes that change expression — blue normally, red/green for status
- Build: Slim humanoid proportions, 5' tall, sleek white and silver body panels
- Distinguishing features: Joints visible, panel seams, TechMedix-compatible ports on back

**State changes**:
- **Broken**: Slumped posture, dim/flickering red eyes, sparks from joints, limp arm
- **Diagnosis mode**: Chest panel open revealing internal circuits, diagnostic lights flashing
- **Repaired**: Standing tall, bright blue eyes, smooth movements, satisfied hum

**Costume**: None — the robot IS the body. Sleek white/silver anime-styled mecha design.

**Color palette**: White (#F0F4F8), silver (#C0C5CE), blue LED (#00B4D8), red alert (#EF4444)

---

## Character 4: The Robot's Pet Bot (optional - Bit)

**Role**: Support/Comic Relief (Role D)
**Description**: A small floating orb-like companion bot that belongs to the humanoid robot. Think BB-8 meets a Pokemon. Expresses emotions through bleeps and movements. Helps slightly during the repair.

**Color palette**: Yellow (#FBBF24), orange (#F59E0B)
