# Knowledge Comic: The TechMedix Repair

**Concept**: A person's humanoid robot breaks down unexpectedly at their home/workshop.
They call BlackCat Robotics for support. A TechMedix service agent arrives to diagnose
and repair the robot, demonstrating professional robotics repair service.

**Key elements**:
- Customer with broken humanoid robot
- BlackCat Robotics / TechMedix brand
- Professional repair service scenario
- Technical but accessible

**Tone**: Old-school anime style, warm, slightly dramatic problem → solution arc
