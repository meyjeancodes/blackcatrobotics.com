How It Works

From charger to grid in four steps.

01
🔌
Connect Hardware
Any OCPP-compliant charger onboards to Grid in minutes. Firmware is deployed OTA. No truck roll required.
OCPP 1.6 + 2.0.1 → Node registered
02
🗓
Schedule Charging
Grid's AI engine analyzes fleet departure times, grid prices, and charger capacity. Vehicles charge at the lowest cost window without missing dispatch.
Fleet plan → Smart schedule → Executed
03
💸
Bill Automatically
Every session is logged per vehicle, per driver, per fleet account. Invoices generate automatically. Disputes resolve with session telemetry.
Session telemetry → Invoice → Exported
04
📈
Trade Energy
When vehicles sit idle, Grid aggregates their battery capacity and bids into wholesale energy markets. Revenue flows back to the fleet operator.
Idle capacity → Grid bid → Revenue split
