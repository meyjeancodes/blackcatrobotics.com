Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us through forms on our website (name, email, company), robot fleet telemetry data under service agreements, and standard web analytics via Plausible (privacy-first, no cookies).

How We Use It

Fleet data is used exclusively to provide TechMedix diagnostics. Contact information is used to respond to inquiries and deliver service updates. We do not sell personal data.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf (Formspree for forms, Plausible for analytics, Vercel for hosting)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

Data Retention

Form submissions are retained for 2 years for business records. Fleet telemetry is retained per service agreement terms. Analytics data is retained per Plausible's privacy policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and access controls.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
