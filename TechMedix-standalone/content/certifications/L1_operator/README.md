# L1 Operator Certification

**Job Value Range:** $280 - $350 per job
**Prerequisites:** None — entry level
**Written Exam:** 30 questions, 75% passing, 45 minutes
**Practical:** Required — assessor sign-off on all safety domains

## What L1 Unlocks

- TechMedix dispatch eligibility for routine maintenance jobs
- Access to the BCR marketplace (entry tier)
- Authority to perform visual inspections, battery swaps, and basic maintenance
- Permission to log and escalate P1/P2 alerts in TechMedix

## What L1 Does Not Unlock

- Actuator disassembly (requires L2)
- CAN bus or ROS 2 diagnostics (requires L2)
- Multi-platform assignments (requires L3)
- Team leadership or enterprise contracts (requires L4+)

## Study Path

1. `curriculum.md` — five sections, study in order

## Estimated Study Time

- Self-study: 20-30 hours
- Lab time: 8-12 hours (with assessor)
- Total: 30-45 hours before sitting the exam
