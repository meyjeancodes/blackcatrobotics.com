# L4 Systems Engineer Certification

**Job Value Range:** $1,200 - $1,800 per job
**Prerequisites:** L3 Specialist + 12 months documented multi-platform field experience
**Written Exam:** 40 questions, 82% passing, 90 minutes
**Practical:** Required

## What L4 Unlocks

- Fleet architecture design and enterprise system integrations
- Weibull failure analysis for reliability engineering
- Spare parts EOQ (Economic Order Quantity) optimization
- Team leadership — training and supervising L1/L2 technicians
- Enterprise contract eligibility (fleet operators with 20+ robots)
- FMEA escalation authority — can authorize immediate robot suspension

## Estimated Study Time

- Self-study: 60-80 hours
- Lab time: 20-25 hours
