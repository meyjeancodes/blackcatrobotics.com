PROGRAM 1: HABITAT — Autonomous Construction Platform

The United States faces a documented housing shortfall exceeding four million units. Traditional construction capacity cannot close this gap at required speed or cost. HABITAT offers a structurally different approach.

HABITAT uses autonomous Construct.Bot fleets to execute the full construction lifecycle — from AI-generated CAD blueprint to permitted, solar-integrated, move-in ready structures — without a general contracting crew. Key characteristics:

- Build targets of 72 hours for small residential structures
- Net-zero energy integration from the foundation up, including solar panels, battery storage, and rainwater systems
- Scalable to multi-family and light commercial structures
- Materials sourced and sequenced automatically; no manual procurement process

We believe HABITAT is well-positioned to address:
- Workforce and affordable housing shortfalls in rural and underserved areas
- Emergency and disaster recovery housing at scale
- State and municipal housing production mandates where traditional methods have underperformed

We are actively seeking government partnerships, land access agreements, pilot program commitments, and procurement discussions at the state, county, and municipal level.

PROGRAM 2: TechMedix — Autonomous Fleet Maintenance Infrastructure

As government agencies and contractors expand the use of autonomous systems — drones for inspection, robots for logistics, AMRs for facility operations — the need for standardized maintenance infrastructure grows accordingly.

TechMedix provides:
- Predictive diagnostics for 14+ autonomous platforms (humanoids, drones, delivery robots, industrial AMRs)
- 48-hour failure prediction lead time
- Automated technician dispatch via a certified field network
- Full audit trail and compliance reporting for FAA, OSHA, and ISO 10218 requirements

TechMedix is applicable to any agency or contractor operating autonomous systems at scale. We are prepared to provide demonstrations, pilot programs, and procurement documentation suitable for government evaluation processes.
